## Weekly Audit Focus Areas

The AI Employee should analyze and report on:

### Revenue Health
- Total earned this week vs. target
- Outstanding invoices (aging report)
- New proposals sent
- Conversion rate (proposals → signed deals)

### Operational Health
- Task completion rate
- Bottlenecks (tasks taking > expected time)
- Client response times
- Project deadline adherence

### Cost Optimization
- Subscription utilization review
- Unusual expenses flagged
- ROI on tools/services
- Waste identification

### Strategic Insights
- Which clients are most profitable per hour?
- Which services have highest margins?
- Where is time being wasted?
- Opportunities being missed?
